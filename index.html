About Me

Who am I?

Hi, I'm Danielle. I am a technical product manager specializing in digital products and e-commerce.

I have five years' experience in technical startup and have helped establish and grow companies from seed funding to IPOs.

Coming from a non-technical background in the highly competitive millennial age, I hope to help and inspire others who want to gain technical expertise but do not know where to start.

How am I different?

My greatest asset is my ability to enthusiastically take on any challenge to produce impactful results.

I am a champion of being the voice of the consumer, imagining every possible angle of an approach to a product, gathering customer feedback and pain points, and executing on ideas from conception to QA.

I enjoy throwing myself into the unknown, the messiest of problems, making sense and order of it to optimize and drive business growth.

What are my accomplishments?

  • Implementing an agile engineering approach and roadmap resulting in increased company productivity 300% and $5,000 overall net revenue in preparation for Series A fundraising round

  • Solving a nuanced inventory problem through algorithm enhancements, coupled with building an external facing tool to create an additional $50K in monthly recurring revenue and ~10K additional purchasable products per month for consumers on site.

  • Managing the lifecycle of an operational tool, cutting out customer serviceâ€™s role in the process and saving over $200K in employment expenses.

  • Ideating, wire-framing and managing the entire product development of a brand-new tool to manage the Credit Recovery process generating over $1MM in recovered losses in the first quarter post-launch.
